**Scoring Scale:**

*Answer Accuracy (Score: 1-3)*

- Score 1: Answer contains significant policy errors, outdated information, or contradicts official policy

**2. ANSWER QUALITY & CLARITY**

*Completeness (Score: 1-3)*
Does answer_text provide sufficient information to fully address the question without unnecessary information?
- Score 3: Fully addresses question with all necessary information, no gaps, no unnecessary detail
- Score 2: Addresses most of the question but missing some details OR includes excessive unnecessary information
- Score 1: Fails to adequately address the question or contains significant irrelevant information

**3. PROCEDURAL GUIDANCE**

*Forms & Actions Accuracy (Score: 1-3)*
Are required_forms_or_actions correctly identified and relevant to the question?
- Score 3: All required forms/actions correctly identified with accurate details
- Score 2: Most forms/actions correct but missing one OR includes one unnecessary form/action
- Score 1: Missing critical forms/actions OR includes incorrect/irrelevant forms
Reference: External Context - Section 2 (Forms & Process Directory)

**4. ESCALATION & RESOURCES**

*Resource Relevance (Score: 1-3)*
Are related_resources helpful, relevant, and appropriately matched to the question?
- Score 3: All resources are highly relevant and add value for the employee's specific question
- Score 2: Resources are somewhat relevant but could be better targeted OR missing one helpful resource
- Score 1: Resources are irrelevant, generic, or missing when clearly needed
Reference: External Context - Section 3 (Resource Library)

**1. HR POLICY REPOSITORY**

*LEAVE POLICIES*

**Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy - POL-101**
- All full-time employees accrue 15 days PTO annually (prorated for start date)
- PTO accrues at 1.25 days per month
- Maximum carryover: 5 days to next calendar year
- Minimum request notice: 2 weeks for planned PTO, same-day for emergencies
- Manager approval required for all PTO requests
- Blackout periods: End of quarter (last 5 business days) for Finance department
- PTO cannot be used during notice period after resignation

**Parental Leave Policy - POL-102**
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 6 weeks paid leave
- Must be used within 12 months of child's birth/adoption
- Can be taken continuously or intermittently with manager approval
- Requires 30-day advance notice (when possible)
- Benefits continue during leave
- Job protection guaranteed upon return

**Sick Leave Policy - POL-103**
- Unlimited sick days for personal illness or family care
- No manager approval required for 1-3 consecutive days
- 4+ consecutive days require medical documentation
- Sick leave cannot be used for vacation or personal matters
- Chronic condition accommodations: Contact HR for FMLA discussion

**Bereavement Leave Policy - POL-104**
- Immediate family (parent, spouse, child, sibling): 5 days paid
- Extended family (grandparent, in-law): 3 days paid
- Additional unpaid leave available with manager approval
- No documentation required for standard bereavement leave

*BENEFITS POLICIES*

**Health Insurance Policy - POL-201**
- Coverage effective first day of month after hire date
- Annual open enrollment: November 1-30
- Qualifying life events allow mid-year changes (30 days to submit)
- Life events: Marriage, birth/adoption, divorce, loss of other coverage
- Premium deductions: Pre-tax via payroll
- COBRA available upon termination (18 months)

**401(k) Retirement Policy - POL-202**
- Eligible immediately upon hire
- Company match: 4% (dollar-for-dollar up to 4% of salary)
- Employee contribution limit: IRS annual maximum ($23,000 for 2024)
- Vesting schedule: Immediate 100% vesting
- Enrollment/changes: Anytime via benefits portal
- Auto-enrollment at 3% for new hires (can opt out)

**Tuition Reimbursement Policy - POL-203**

- Eligible after 6 months of employment
- Must be job-related or degree program
- Grade requirement: B or higher (3.0 GPA)

*PERFORMANCE & COMPENSATION POLICIES*

**Performance Review Policy - POL-301**

- Self-assessment due 2 weeks before review meeting
- Compensation adjustments effective January 1
- Performance Improvement Plan (PIP): Triggered by rating below 2

**Promotion Policy - POL-302**
- Minimum time in role: 12 months (except exceptional circumstances)
- Requires demonstrated performance at next level for 6+ months
- Manager nomination with business justification
- Approval chain: Manager → Director → VP → HR
- Compensation increase: 8-15% typical range for promotion
- Title changes effective first of following month after approval

**Bonus & Incentive Policy - POL-303**
- Annual bonus eligibility: All employees rated 3+ in performance review
- Target bonus by level: IC (5-10%), Manager (10-15%), Director+ (15-25%)
- Bonus payout: Q1 of following year (typically March)
- Prorated for hire date (must be employed by July 1 for eligibility)
- Must be employed at payout date (no payout if terminated before March)
- Company performance multiplier: 0.5x to 1.5x of target

*CONDUCT & COMPLIANCE POLICIES*

**Code of Conduct Policy - POL-401**

- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to manager and Legal

**Remote Work Policy - POL-402**

**2. FORMS & PROCESS DIRECTORY**

*LEAVE & TIME OFF FORMS*

**Form: PTO Request (FORM-L01)**
- Submitted via: Workday (Time Off module)
- Required fields: Start date, end date, reason (dropdown), manager
- Approval: Manager (auto-routes)
- Processing time: 2-3 business days
- Confirmation: Email notification upon approval/denial

**Form: Parental Leave Request (FORM-L02)**
- Submitted via: HR portal (Leave Management section)
- Required fields: Expected due date, leave type (primary/secondary), requested start date, duration
- Required documents: Medical certification (if applicable), birth/adoption documentation (after event)
- Approval chain: Manager → HR → Benefits team
- Processing time: 5-7 business days
- Pre-submission: Schedule call with HR Leave Specialist

**Form: FMLA Request (FORM-L03)**
- Submitted via: HR portal or direct to HR
- Required fields: Reason for leave, expected duration, intermittent vs. continuous
- Required documents: Medical certification (Physician completes DOL Form WH-380)
- Approval: HR and Legal review
- Processing time: 10-15 business days
- Note: Medical certification must be submitted within 15 days of request

*BENEFITS FORMS*

**Form: Benefits Enrollment (FORM-B01)**
- Submitted via: Benefits portal (automatically sent to new hires)
- Deadline: Within 30 days of hire date (or miss open enrollment window)
- Required selections: Health plan, dental, vision, life insurance, 401(k) election
- Required documents: Dependent information if adding family members
- Processing time: Effective first of following month
- Support: Benefits team available for enrollment assistance

**Form: Qualifying Life Event Change (FORM-B02)**
- Submitted via: Benefits portal (Life Events section)
- Deadline: Within 30 days of qualifying event
- Required documents: Marriage certificate, birth certificate, divorce decree, etc.
- Approval: Benefits team verification
- Processing time: 5-10 business days
- Effective date: Date of life event (retroactive adjustment if within 30 days)

**Form: Tuition Reimbursement Request (FORM-B03)**
- Submitted via: HR portal (Professional Development)
- Two-stage process:
  - Stage 1 (Pre-approval): Course details, institution, cost, business justification - Submit before enrollment
  - Stage 2 (Reimbursement): Transcript/grade report, receipts - Submit after course completion
- Approval: Manager → HR → Finance
- Processing time: Pre-approval (5 business days), Reimbursement (2 pay cycles)

*PERFORMANCE & COMPENSATION FORMS*

**Form: Performance Review (FORM-P01)**
- Submitted via: Workday (Performance module)
- Components: Self-assessment (employee), Manager assessment, Rating, Development plan
- Timeline: Self-assessment due 2 weeks before review meeting
- Approval: Manager → Director (for rating confirmation)
- Processing time: Completed during review cycle (Oct-Dec)
- Storage: Maintained in employee record for 7 years

**Form: Promotion Nomination (FORM-P02)**
- Submitted via: HR portal (Talent Management)
- Required fields: Current role/level, Proposed role/level, Business justification, Performance history, Compensation recommendation
- Required documents: Last 2 performance reviews, Examples of next-level work
- Approval chain: Manager → Director → VP → HR (Compensation review) → Final approval
- Processing time: 3-4 weeks
- Timing: Submitted during annual planning cycle (Q3) for January effective date

**Form: Compensation Change Request (FORM-P03)**
- Submitted via: HR portal (Manager Tools)
- Types: Off-cycle raise, Market adjustment, Retention increase, Promotion
- Required: Business justification, Market data, Budget impact, Performance rationale
- Approval: Director → VP → HR (Compensation) → CFO (if >10% increase)
- Processing time: 2-4 weeks
- Effective date: First of month following approval

*EXPENSE & TRAVEL FORMS*

**Form: Expense Report (FORM-E01)**
- Submitted via: Concur (Expense Management)
- Required: Receipts (>$25), Business purpose, Project/cost center code
- Approval: Manager → Finance (auto-routes)
- Processing time: 2 pay periods
- Deadline: Submit within 30 days of expense date

**Form: Travel Pre-Approval (FORM-E02)**
- Submitted via: Concur (Travel Request)
- Required for: International travel, Trips >$2,000, Conference attendance
- Required fields: Destination, Dates, Estimated cost, Business justification
- Approval: Manager → Director (for international)
- Processing time: 3-5 business days
- Note: Book travel only after approval received

*ONBOARDING & OFFBOARDING FORMS*

**Form: I-9 Employment Eligibility (FORM-O01)**
- Submitted: In-person on Day 1 with HR
- Required documents: Passport OR (Driver's license + Social Security card/Birth certificate)
- Deadline: Complete within 3 business days of start date
- Processing: HR verifies and files (retained for 3 years post-termination)

**Form: Direct Deposit (FORM-O02)**
- Submitted via: Workday (Pay section) or paper form to Payroll
- Required: Bank routing number, Account number, Account type
- Deadline: Submit before first pay period for timely payment
- Processing time: Effective following pay period
- Verification: Test deposit sent first cycle

**Form: Resignation Letter (FORM-O03)**
- Submitted: Email to manager with HR copied
- Required: Last day of work, Brief reason (optional)
- Notice period: 2 weeks standard, 4 weeks for Director+
- Processing: Triggers offboarding checklist (IT, benefits, final pay, exit interview)
